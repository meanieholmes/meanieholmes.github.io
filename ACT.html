Ants Climbing Tree

Ingredients

  • 2 bundles dry vermicelli/glass noodles
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced ginger
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 tablespoons spicy fermented bean sauce/paste
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • ½ teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ¼ cup chopped green onions

    Directions

    1. Soak the dry noodles in cold water for 10 minutes. Rinse, drain and set aside. In a wok over medium heat, add the oil and minced ginger and garlic. Cook the ginger and garlic for about a minute, and add the spicy bean sauce. Let cook for one more minute.

    2. Add the ground pork. Stir-fry until the meat is cooked through.

    3. Add the chicken broth, sugar, and soy sauce.

    4. Bring everything to a boil. Once boiling, add noodles and green onion. Cook an additional 1-2 minutes and serve over steamed rice.
