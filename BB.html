Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¾ cup butter
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 3 large eggs eggs
  • 6 medium (7" to 7-7/8" long)s very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 ½ cups all-purposef lour
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

    • Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C). Line cupcake tin with papers.
      2. >
    2. In a large bowl, cream butter and 3 cups sugar. Mix in eggs, mashed bananas, sour cream, vanilla and cinnamon. Mix in salt, baking soda and flour. Stir in nuts. Divide into cupcake pan.

    3. Bake for 18 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
